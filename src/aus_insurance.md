
Many types of insurance are offered by financial institutions. 

Some types of insurance to be covered here:

- Health insurance
- Life insurance
- Travel insurance
- Auto insurance
- Home content insurance
- Income protection insurance
- Credit insurance

InsuranceWatch is a service that compares insurance products in Australia (<a href="http://insurancewatch.com.au/" target="_blank">link</a>) and contains some useful information.
