The point with these sites is that they want you to sign as many referrals. It appears that you are making loads of money doing nothing but, as with any pyramidal scheme, this is not bound to work for long.

Some sites offer click exchange and impression exchange, so to speak. Some of the above sites include that service, some are dedicated to such a service only. The system works in a way that you browse listed sites, manually or automatically, and in exchange get to place your web pages into the system. The second table contains some links to the sites that offer this kind of service, for illustration of the concept.
